# Core Insights

## Three realities define why AuditPal exists:

* **The logic gap is still the biggest threat.** $2.1 billion was lost in 2025 Q1 alone. Access control exploits accounted for the majority. Static tools and legacy AI miss complex business logic flaws. The OWASP Smart Contract Top 10 for 2026 puts access control and logic vulnerabilities at the top of the list, exactly where they have been for years.
* **Traditional audits cannot scale with on-chain velocity.** Top firms make teams wait 1 to 3 weeks. Markets move in hours. RWAs and stablecoins are hitting record volumes. AI software engineering tools are producing contracts faster than any fixed team of reviewers can clear. The supply of secure contracts is falling behind demand.
* **Post-launch security is not optional.** Contracts stay deployed for years. New integrations, drifted assumptions, and novel attack patterns emerge constantly after launch. A one-time report cannot protect a live system. Continuous verification is the only model that makes sense.
