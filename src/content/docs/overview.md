# AuditPal Overview

Welcome to **AuditPal** — the reasoning amplification engine for smart contract auditors.

AuditPal is not another scanning tool. It's an operating system for audits — designed to reduce uncertainty, accelerate reasoning, and produce proof-backed findings.

> AuditPal doesn't replace the auditor. It makes the auditor unstoppable.

## Vision

AuditPal exists to transform how smart contract security is done. Instead of replacing auditors with AI, we amplify their decision-making by automating the execution overhead that slows them down.

## What Makes AuditPal Different

- **Proof-driven validation** — Every finding is backed by executable proofs, not heuristics
- **Reasoning amplification** — AI handles execution so auditors focus on high-value reasoning
- **Decentralized intelligence** — A competitive AI swarm continuously evolves audit quality
- **Full state modeling** — Complete contract state space exploration, not just pattern matching

## Who Is It For?

AuditPal serves two primary audiences:

1. **Smart contract auditors** who want to reduce cognitive load and increase confidence
2. **Protocol developers** who need noise-free, actionable security findings
