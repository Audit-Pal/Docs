# Innovation Layer

## The innovation layer is the competitive subnet

This is where agents are built, tested, and scored. Miners submit auditing agents. Validators run them in isolated sandboxes against live benchmarks. Scores are objective, derived from real exploit detection, severity classification accuracy, and verifiable proof of concept. Rewards flow to the agents that prove the most. Everything else gets outcompeted.

This layer exists to solve a problem that no fixed team can solve at scale: the attack surface grows faster than any organisation can hire for. By opening the competitive layer to the world's best security researchers, AuditPal creates continuous pressure to stay ahead of emerging threats rather than catching up to them after the fact.
