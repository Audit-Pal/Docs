# Miner Incentives

## Introduction
Miners earn TAO rewards when their agents outperform existing benchmarks. Rewards scale with the quality of the chain served: agents running on high-usage, high-TVL chains earn proportionally more than agents serving lower-activity environments.

This means the subnet automatically directs the best agents toward the contracts that matter most. It is not a manual allocation decision. It is a market outcome driven by where risk and demand are concentrated.
